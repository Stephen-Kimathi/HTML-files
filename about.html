About Us

Our Story

Bloom Valley Nursery was founded in 1998 by gardening enthusiasts Maria and Robert Chen. What started as a small family operation has grown into the region's premier destination for quality plants and gardening supplies.

Our mission is to inspire and support our community in creating beautiful, sustainable gardens. We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy plants and expert guidance, whether they're planting their first herb garden or designing a complete landscape.

We source our plants from trusted local growers and use sustainable practices throughout our operations. Our commitment to quality and customer service has made us a trusted partner for thousands of satisfied gardeners.
